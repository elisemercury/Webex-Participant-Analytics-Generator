Webex Participant Analytics Generator (WPAG) v1.0

Basic Usage

A host of a Webex meeting can generate and download participant data of its Webex meetings.
Participant data can be generated for a meeting occurence, or a recurring series. For recurring meeting series, participant data will be generated for the most recent meeting in the series.
Meeting data can be fetched until up to a maximum of 1 month after the meeting occured.
Login and authentication via Cisco Webex is required before WPAG can be accessed.
